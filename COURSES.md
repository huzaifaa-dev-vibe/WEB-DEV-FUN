## How to Pick

- **Just starting?** → The Odin Project or FreeCodeCamp.
- **Already coding, want better?** → Frontend Masters subscription + one paid course in your weak area.
- **Job-hunting?** → Tech Interview Handbook + NeetCode + ByteByteGo.
- **Need to learn a specific thing?** → Find the official docs tutorial first. If insufficient, find the top course on that topic.

---

## Anti-Advice

- Don't hoard courses. Take one. Finish it. Ship a project.
- Don't watch on 2x speed. You're not learning, you're feeling productive.
- Tutorial hell is real. After every chapter, build something not in the tutorial.
- Don't pay for a course until you've finished a free one in the same area.
