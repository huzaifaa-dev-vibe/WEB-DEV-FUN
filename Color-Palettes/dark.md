## Dark mode principles

1. **Don't use pure black** (`#000`) for surface. Use `#0a0a0a` or `#0f0f0f`. Pure black causes eye strain.
2. **Don't invert white text.** Use `#fafafa` or `#e5e5e5` — easier on eyes.
3. **Reduce contrast slightly** from light mode.
4. **Use a tinted dark** (warm/cool/neutral), not pure gray.
5. **Adjust accent** — may need to brighten for dark.
6. **Shadows** are less visible — use borders instead.
