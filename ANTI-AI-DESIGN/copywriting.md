# Anti-AI-Design: Copywriting

> Real copy. Not lorem. Not generic. Not AI-sounding.

---

## The AI copy tells

1. **"Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet"**
2. **"We provide innovative solutions"**
3. **"Empower your team with..."**
4. **"In today's fast-paced world..."**
5. **"Unlock the power of..."**
6. **"Cutting-edge" / "game-changing" / "revolutionary"**
7. **"Seamless" / "robust" / "scalable" / "enterprise-grade"** (without specifics)
8. **Emoji in headlines** 🚀
9. **"Built with AI" / "AI-powered" / "Next-generation"**
10. **Verbosity.** 3 paragraphs to say what could be 1 sentence.

---

## Human copy principles

### 1. Specific over generic
- ❌ "Build amazing things"
- ✅ "Ship a marketing site in 2 hours, not 2 weeks."

### 2. Concrete numbers
- ❌ "Faster development"
- ✅ "3x faster deploys"

### 3. Active voice
- ❌ "Your data is secured by us"
- ✅ "We encrypt your data at rest."

### 4. Short
- ❌ 3-sentence tagline
- ✅ 1-sentence tagline

### 5. Talk like a person
- ❌ "Facilitate the deployment of mission-critical applications"
- ✅ "Ship apps without breaking prod."

### 6. Verb-led CTAs
- ❌ "Click here" / "Learn more" / "Submit"
- ✅ "Start free trial" / "Read the docs" / "Send invite"

### 7. No filler
- ❌ "We are a team of passionate developers who believe in the power of..."
- ✅ "We make X."

### 8. Honest
- ❌ "The best tool for X"
- ✅ "Built for teams who need X"

### 9. Voice consistent
- Pick a tone (formal / casual / technical / playful) and stay.
- Don't be casual in hero and formal in docs.

### 10. Write the headline you'd want
- Don't write what "sounds good". Write what you'd say to a friend.

## Hero copy formulas

### The "what + who + why" formula
- What: "Ship a marketing site in 2 hours."
- Who: "Built for non-technical founders."
- Why: "Stop hiring agencies for v1."

### The "claim + proof" formula
- Claim: "The fastest way to ship a SaaS."
- Proof: "10,000+ teams shipped with us last month."

### The "negative + positive" formula
- Negative: "Stop wasting time on boilerplate."
- Positive: "Start shipping features."

### The "specific outcome" formula
- "Cut your deploy time from 30 min to 30 sec."

## CTA copy

| Bad | Good |
|---|---|
| Click here | Start free trial |
| Submit | Send invite |
| Learn more | Read the docs |
| Get started | Build your first app |
| Sign up | Create account |
| Buy now | Get 14 days free |

---

## Section copy

### Hero
- Headline: 1 sentence. Specific outcome.
- Subhead: 1 sentence. Who it's for.
- CTA: action verb.

### Features
- Headline: 1 sentence. Not "Features".
- Each feature: name + 1 sentence (specific).

### Testimonials
- Real quote. Real attribution.

### Pricing
- Don't apologize for price.
- Show what's included.
- Compare to alternative (cost of NOT using).

### FAQ
- Real questions, not strawmen.
- Concise answers.

## Examples (re-write these)

### Bad (AI-generated)
> "Welcome to Acme Inc. We are a passionate team of innovators dedicated to empowering businesses with cutting-edge solutions. Our mission is to revolutionize the way you work by leveraging the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning. With our robust and scalable platform, you can unlock new opportunities and drive growth. Click here to learn more about how we can transform your business."

### Good (human)
> "Acme helps you ship a marketing site in 2 hours.
>
> Built for non-technical founders who'd rather build product than fight with templates.
>
> [Start free trial]"

---

## When in doubt

- Cut it.
- Be specific.
- Be honest.
- Write what you'd say to a friend.
