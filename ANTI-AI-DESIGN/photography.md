# Anti-AI-Design: Photography

> Real photos. Or no photos.

---

## The AI tells

1. "Diverse team laughing at laptop" stock.
2. Unsplash random mountains.
3. Stock photos of handshakes.
4. Same photo on 1000 other sites.
5. Photos that don't relate to the content.
6. Generic "office space" stock.

---

## Human approach

### Option 1: Real photos
- Real product screenshots.
- Real team photos.
- Real customer photos (with permission).
- Real office / workspace.

### Option 2: No photos
- Use typography, color, layout instead.
- Often stronger than mediocre stock.

### Option 3: Custom illustrations
- Even simple geometric / hand-drawn beats stock.
- Recolorable to match brand.
- Unique to you.

### Option 4: Real product UI
- Show your actual product.
- Better than any stock photo.

---

## When stock is acceptable

- Background texture (very subtle, abstract).
- News article header (relevant to the story).
- Generic blog post header (use sparingly).
- When you genuinely need a photo and can't get a real one — but acknowledge it.

Even then, prefer:
- Uncommon stock (not the first page of Unsplash).
- Paid stock (less reused).
- AI-generated (less recognizable than overused stock, but be careful — AI photos have their own tells).

---

## Photo guidelines

### Real product screenshots
- High resolution.
- Real data (not "Lorem ipsum" in the screenshot).
- Properly cropped.
- Add a subtle shadow / border.
- Responsive (different crops for mobile/desktop).

### Real team photos
- Casual, not stuffy headshots.
- Same background / style for consistency.
- Permission from each person.
- Optional: link to their LinkedIn/Twitter.

### Real customer photos
- Same as team photos.
- Link to case study.

### Real office photos
- Show personality.
- Not just "open plan office".
- Real details (whiteboards, plants, etc.).

---

## What to avoid

- Stock photos of:
  - "Business team meeting".
  - "Diverse team laughing at laptop".
  - "Handshake".
  - "Person pointing at chart".
  - "Mountain landscape" (unless travel/outdoors).
- AI-generated photos of:
  - "Person" with weird hands / eyes / teeth.
  - "Office" with impossible geometry.
- Random Unsplash photos everyone has seen.

---

## Alternative to photos

### Illustration
- Custom (even simple).
- Recolor to match brand.
- Storyset, unDraw, Blush (customizable).

### Pure design
- Big typography.
- Color blocks.
- Geometric shapes.
- Negative space.

Often stronger than mediocre photos.
