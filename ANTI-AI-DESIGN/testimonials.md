# Anti-AI-Design: Testimonials

> No fake testimonials. Real or omit.

---

## The AI tell

- 5 stars + stock photo + generic quote.
- "Sarah Johnson, CEO at TechCorp" with a model's photo.
- "This product changed my life!" — John D.
- Three identical testimonial cards in a row.

---

## Human approach

### Option 1: Real testimonials
- Real customer quote.
- Real name + role + company.
- Real photo (with permission) OR no photo.
- Link to case study.
- Optional: real video.

### Option 2: No testimonials section
- If you don't have real ones, omit.
- Don't fake it.

### Option 3: Usage stats instead
- "10,000+ teams ship with us"
- "2M+ deploys this month"
- One number beats three fake quotes.

### Option 4: Single big quote
- One massive pull quote.
- Attribution.
- Link to full case study.

### Option 5: Customer logos
- Grid of 6-8 real customer logos.
- One line: "Trusted by..."
- No fake quotes.

---

## What makes a testimonial real

1. **Specific outcome.** "We cut deploy time from 30 min to 30 sec."
2. **Concrete number.** Not "we love it" but "we shipped 14 features last sprint".
3. **Real attribution.** Name, role, company, link.
4. **Honest tone.** Not "absolutely incredible amazing".
5. **Permission.** Customer agreed to be quoted.

---

## Anti-patterns

### ❌ Three identical cards
```
[Card 1: stock photo, 5 stars, "Game-changing!"]
[Card 2: stock photo, 5 stars, "Revolutionary!"]
[Card 3: stock photo, 5 stars, "Incredible!"]
```

### ❌ Vague quotes
> "This product is amazing and changed everything."

### ❌ Stock photo + generic name
> "Sarah Johnson, CEO"
> [photo of model from Unsplash]

### ❌ Auto-rotating carousel
- Don't make users wait for the next quote.
- Show all (or one big one).

---

## Good patterns

### ✅ Single big quote
> "We cut deploy time from 30 min to 30 sec. Acme paid for itself in week one."
>
> — Jane Doe, VP Eng at RealCompany
> [Read the case study →]

### ✅ Customer logos grid
```
Trusted by 10,000+ teams
[logo] [logo] [logo] [logo]
[logo] [logo] [logo] [logo]
```

### ✅ One stat + one quote
```
2M+ deploys this month

"We ship 10x faster since switching to Acme."
— John Smith, CTO at Startup
```
