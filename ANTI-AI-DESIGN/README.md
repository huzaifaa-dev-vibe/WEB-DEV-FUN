# ANTI-AI-DESIGN

> The folder that prevents your work from looking AI-generated. Read this BEFORE generating any UI.

This folder exists because LLMs have a recognizable "look" — and it's becoming a tell that cheapens everything. The principles here teach you to design like a human.

---

## Purpose

Stop your websites from looking like every other AI-generated site. Make them look like a human designed them — because a human did, with AI as a tool, not the other way around.

## Prerequisites

- [UI/](../UI/), [UX/](../UX/), [CSS/](../CSS/), [Typography/](../Typography/).

## Learning Outcome

Your UI doesn't trigger the "AI made this" reaction. It looks intentional, considered, and human.

## The 12 Tells of AI-Generated UI

These are the patterns that scream "AI made this". Avoid them.

1. **Centered hero + 2 CTAs + 3 feature cards.** The default AI layout. → [hero-alternatives.md](hero-alternatives.md)
2. **Purple-to-pink gradient.** The AI color signature. → [color.md](color.md)
3. **`rounded-2xl` on everything.** Cards, buttons, inputs, images — all same radius. → [radii.md](radii.md)
4. **Emoji icons.** 🚀⚡🎯 as feature icons. → [icons.md](icons.md)
5. **Lorem ipsum or generic copy.** "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" or "We provide innovative solutions." → [copywriting.md](copywriting.md)
6. **3-card "Features" grid with icon + headline + 1 sentence.** Every AI demo ever.
7. **Generic testimonials with stock photos and 5 stars.** → [testimonials.md](testimonials.md)
8. **Fade-in-up on every section.** → [motion.md](motion.md)
9. **"Powered by AI" badge.** Cheapens real AI work.
10. **Glassmorphism everywhere.** `backdrop-blur` on cards over gradient.
11. **Identical spacing rhythm.** All sections same padding, no asymmetry.
12. **Photography of "diverse team laughing at laptop".** Stock photos everyone has seen.

## The 10 Human-Design Principles

→ Full version in [principles.md](principles.md). Summary:

1. **Intention over convention.** Every choice has a reason.
2. **Asymmetry creates hierarchy.** Don't center everything.
3. **Constraint creates voice.** Pick a palette and stay.
4. **Real copy beats placeholder.** Always.
5. **Real photos beat stock.** Always. (Or no photos.)
6. **One typeface, used well, beats five.**
7. **Motion explains state.** Not decoration.
8. **Imperfection is human.** Slight rotation, hand-drawn elements, asymmetric spacing.
9. **Density varies.** Not every section breathes the same.
10. **Voice is consistent.** Pick a tone and stay.

## The Anti-AI-Design Checklist (run before shipping)

→ Full version in [checklist.md](checklist.md). Summary:

- [ ] Hero is not centered + 2 CTAs + 3 cards.
- [ ] No purple-to-pink gradient by default.
- [ ] Radii are mixed intentionally.
- [ ] No emoji icons.
- [ ] Copy is real, not lorem.
- [ ] Testimonials are real or omitted.
- [ ] Motion has purpose.
- [ ] Photography is real or absent.
- [ ] Spacing varies.
- [ ] Layout isn't a generic grid.
- [ ] Site has personality.
- [ ] A human designer would not immediately say "AI made this".

## AI Instructions (when generating UI)

1. **Read every file in this folder BEFORE writing JSX/HTML.**
2. **Pick a hero layout from [hero-alternatives.md](hero-alternatives.md).**
3. **Pick a palette from [Color-Palettes/](../Color-Palettes/) — not purple-pink.**
4. **Pick real copy.** No lorem. → [copywriting.md](copywriting.md)
5. **Use Lucide icons**, not emoji.
6. **Mix radii**: 4px / 8px / 16px / full.
7. **Vary section spacing.**
8. **Add one element of imperfection** (slight rotation, asymmetric grid, hand-drawn detail).
9. **Run [checklist.md](checklist.md)** before reporting done.

## Human Notes

The web is being flooded with AI-generated sites that all look the same. They're technically fine but feel cheap. They have no voice. No point of view. No soul.

Human design has:
- **A point of view.** The designer made choices.
- **Imperfections.** Not everything aligns. Not everything is the same size.
- **Voice.** You can tell who made it.
- **Restraint.** Not every section has every feature.
- **Density variation.** Some sections breathe. Others pack.

This folder teaches you to inject those qualities into your work, even when using AI as a tool.
