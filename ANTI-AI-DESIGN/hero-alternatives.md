# Hero Alternatives

> 15 hero layouts that aren't "centered headline + 2 CTAs + 3 feature cards".

The centered hero with two buttons is the most AI-generated layout on the internet. Stop using it as your default.

---

## 1. Asymmetric split (text + product)
- Text left (60%).
- Real product screenshot right (40%).
- Image bleeds off the right edge.

## 2. Editorial magazine cover
- Full-bleed photo.
- Large title overlaid.
- Small subtitle.
- No CTA in hero — CTA below the fold.

## 3. Big number
- One giant number ("$2.4B saved by customers").
- Tiny supporting line.
- Single subtle CTA.

## 4. Interactive demo
- Live interactive widget (calculator, preview, playground).
- Minimal text above.
- "Try it" CTA built into the demo.

## 5. Video background with quote
- Muted autoplay video (10s loop).
- Single customer quote overlaid.
- Small attribution + CTA below.

## 6. Asymmetric grid
- Title top-left, small.
- Massive image bottom-right, bleeds.
- Body copy top-right.
- CTA bottom-left.

## 7. Manifesto
- Long-form prose statement (200 words).
- No CTA in hero — invite to read.
- Single-line link "Read our manifesto →" at bottom.

## 8. Customer logos + claim
- Top: simple claim (1 line).
- Middle: grid of 6-8 customer logos.
- Bottom: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" + 1 CTA.

## 9. Big quote
- Massive pull quote (customer's words).
- Attribution.
- Single CTA: "Read the case study →".

## 10. Product video
- Auto-playing product tour video (muted, 30s loop).
- Title above.
- "Watch 2-min tour" CTA.

## 11. Single-sentence + search
- One sentence: "Find the right tool for the job."
- Big search input.
- No other CTA.

## 12. Comparison table teaser
- "X vs Y" table preview.
- 3 rows visible.
- "See full comparison →" CTA.

## 13. Timeline / journey
- Horizontal or vertical timeline of milestones.
- "Where we're going next" CTA at the end.

## 14. Animated illustration
- Custom animated SVG / Lottie.
- Title overlaid.
- Single CTA.

## 15. Empty space + sentence
- 90% whitespace.
- One sentence, centered (yes, centered, but no other elements).
- Single subtle CTA at bottom.

---

## When the centered hero IS appropriate

- When you're a known brand and the hero is just confirmation.
- When the page is one of many and the hero is consistent across them.
- When the centered hero is intentionally minimalist (like Apple).

For 95% of projects, pick an alternative above.

---

## The "AI hero" diagnosis

If your hero is:
- Centered text.
- Big headline.
- Subtitle.
- Two CTAs (one solid, one outline).
- Three feature cards below.

→ It looks AI-generated. → Replace with one of the alternatives above.

---

## How to pick

Match the layout to the content:
- **Product showcase** → Alternative 1 (split) or 4 (demo).
- **Editorial / brand** → Alternative 2 (magazine) or 7 (manifesto).
- **Trust / social proof** → Alternative 8 (logos) or 9 (quote).
- **Interactive product** → Alternative 4 (demo) or 11 (search).
- **Comparison / buyer intent** → Alternative 12 (table).
- **Brand statement** → Alternative 15 (empty space) or 7 (manifesto).
