# Anti-AI-Design: Before / After

> Bad AI vs good human. Side by side.

---

## Example 1: SaaS Landing Hero

### ❌ AI-generated
```
[Centered]

# Empower Your Business with AI

We provide cutting-edge solutions to transform your workflow.

[Get Started] [Learn More]

🚀  ⚡  🎯

[Card: Fast]  [Card: Secure]  [Card: Scalable]

"We provide innovative solutions."
— Sarah Johnson, CEO

[Purple-pink gradient background]
```

### ✅ Human
```
[Left-aligned, asymmetric]

Ship a marketing site
in 2 hours, not 2 weeks.

Built for non-technical founders who'd rather build
product than fight with templates.

[Start free trial — no card needed]

[Right side: real product screenshot, slight -2deg rotation,
bleeds off right edge]

[Below: real customer logos in a row]
"Trusted by 10,000+ teams"
[Stripe] [Linear] [Vercel] [Notion] [Figma] [Loom]
```

Differences:
- Asymmetric, not centered.
- Specific outcome ("2 hours, not 2 weeks"), not generic.
- One CTA, not two.
- Real product screenshot, not feature cards.
- Real customer logos, not fake testimonials.
- No purple-pink gradient.
- Slight rotation = imperfection.

## Example 2: Features Section

### ❌ AI-generated
```
Features

[Card with 🚀 icon]
Lightning Fast
Built for speed and performance.

[Card with ⚡ icon]
Secure
Enterprise-grade security built in.

[Card with 🎯 icon]
Precise
Get exactly what you need.

[Card with 💡 icon]
Smart
AI-powered insights.
```

### ✅ Human
```
Why teams switch

Most tools give you 100 features you'll never use.
We give you 4 you'll use every day.

[Big number]
2.3 hrs
saved per deploy, on average.

[Real screenshot]
[Image: deploy log showing before/after times]

[Quote, not card]
"Cut our deploy time from 30 min to 30 sec.
Acme paid for itself in week one."
— Jane Doe, VP Eng at RealCompany
[Read the case study →]
```

Differences:
- Honest framing (acknowledge what they don't do).
- One big stat, not 4 cards.
- Real screenshot.
- Real quote (no card, no 5 stars).
- No emoji.
- Asymmetric layout.

## Example 3: Pricing

### ❌ AI-generated
```
Pricing

[Starter - $9/mo]
- 5 users
- 10GB storage
- Email support

[Pro - $29/mo] [MOST POPULAR badge]
- 25 users
- 100GB storage
- Priority support
- Advanced analytics

[Enterprise - Custom]
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited storage
- Dedicated support
- Custom integrations
```

### ✅ Human
```
Pricing

Starts at $29/month. 14-day free trial. No card needed.

[Single column, not 3-tier]

Hobby — Free
For side projects.
- 1 project
- Community support
[Start free]

Pro — $29/month
For indie teams.
- Unlimited projects
- 5 collaborators
- Email support
[Start 14-day trial]

Team — $99/month
For growing teams.
- Everything in Pro
- 20 collaborators
- Priority support
- SSO
[Start 14-day trial]

For enterprise: [Talk to us].
```

Differences:
- Single column (not 3-tier grid with "Most Popular").
- Honest copy ("For side projects").
- No "Unlimited" claims.
- Enterprise is a link, not a card.

## The pattern

| AI-generated | Human-designed |
|---|---|
| Centered | Asymmetric |
| Generic copy | Specific copy |
| 3-card grid | One big thing |
| Stock photos | Real screenshots |
| 5 stars + fake quote | Real quote + link |
| Emoji icons | No icons (or real SVG) |
| `rounded-2xl` everywhere | Mixed radii |
| Purple-pink gradient | Solid color or 2-color gradient |
| `fade-in-up` everywhere | Motion with purpose |
| `py-24` everywhere | Varied spacing |
| Perfect symmetry | One intentional imperfection |
